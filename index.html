About Us

We are a Brisbane based company which is well regarded locally. We are recognised as being able to manage childeren's needs with a professional caring approach. We employ two full-time, in-store qualified optometrists able to diagnose and treat a number of eye related problems. Our optometrists strive to make a positive difference to our health and well-being of our customers, endeavouring to obtain the best visual outcome so that customers realise their full potential in work, study,sport and leisure.

Services

  • Eye Examinations (may be bulk billed)
  • Spectacles (may be bulk billed to private Health Fund optical cover_
  • Diabetic eye examinations
  • Colour Vision assessments
  • Macular Degeneration tests
  • Glaucoma & eye pressure tests
  • Checks for cataract
  • Clearness of vision (myopia, hyperopia, astigmatism)
  • Focusing abilty (Presbyopia)
  • Eye muscle movement and control
  • Amblyopia (lazy eye)
  • Contact lens fitting and management
  • Referrals for eye laser surgery
  • Minig & Industrial Safety Spectales
  • Advice on the correct fitting of good quality sunglasses (both prescription and non-prescription, in both adults and children, particularly in a sporting environment)
